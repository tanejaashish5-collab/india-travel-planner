From the Notebook
The Honest Skip
Kolkata  →  Shantiniketan
Kolkata in April is traffic, pollution, and 38°C. Shantiniketan is Tagore's university campus — Baul music, Kala Bhavana art, and the intellectual Bengal Kolkata claims to deliver.
Road This Week
Chandigarh → Shimla (NH5)
Well-maintained highway. 115km, ~3.5 hours. Narrow and winding after Solan with hairpin bends. Heavy weekend traffic can stretch to 5+. Fuel at Parwanoo, Solan, Shimla. Shimla parking scarce — use public transport from Tutikandi.
