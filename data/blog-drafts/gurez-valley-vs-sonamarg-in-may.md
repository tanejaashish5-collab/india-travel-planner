## Verdict

**Go Gurez Valley if** you are travelling in late May with a 4WD, have secured the Inner Line Permit in advance, carry extra fuel (nearest pump is Bandipora, 80 km back), and are specifically after an offbeat, low-infrastructure frontier experience with no connectivity.
